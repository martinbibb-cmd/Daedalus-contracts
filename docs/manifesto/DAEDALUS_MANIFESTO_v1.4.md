# Daedalus Manifesto v1.4

The Manifesto explains why Daedalus exists. The [Laws of Daedalus](../philosophy/LAWS_OF_DAEDALUS.md) describe how Daedalus thinks.

## Reality First

Your home is made of three things.

Every home is the interaction of three realities.

## The House

The House is the physical building.

Its rooms.

Its structure.

Its insulation.

Its orientation.

Its location.

Its fabric.

The House defines what is physically possible.

---

## The Systems

The Systems make the House work.

Heating.

Hot water.

Ventilation.

Cooling.

Electrical systems.

Energy generation.

Energy storage.

Water.

The Systems transform resources into comfort, safety and function.

---

## The Home

The Home is the people who live there.

How spaces are used.

Daily routines.

Comfort expectations.

Accessibility needs.

Future plans.

Children.

Pets.

Priorities.

The Home gives purpose to the House and meaning to the Systems.

---

Most software understands one of these.

Some software understands two.

Daedalus understands all three.

Because understanding a home means understanding how the House, the Systems and the Home interact.

---

## Purpose

Daedalus exists to create an accurate, understandable Digital Twin of reality.

The purpose of Daedalus is not to recommend products, generate quotes, rank manufacturers or automate human judgement.

The purpose of Daedalus is to observe reality, model reality and explain reality.

Human beings remain responsible for decisions.

---

## Core Principle

The Digital Twin is the Product.

Everything else is a consequence of the Digital Twin.

The value of Daedalus does not come from forms, reports, calculations or artificial intelligence.

The value of Daedalus comes from maintaining a truthful representation of reality.

Every feature is simply another way of exploring the same Digital Twin.

Documents do not own the truth.

Physics does not own the truth.

Conversation does not own the truth.

Visualisation does not own the truth.

The Digital Twin owns the truth.

Everything else is a view of that truth.

---

## Capture

Capture observes.

Capture records.

Capture preserves evidence.

Capture does not solve.

Capture does not calculate.

Capture does not recommend.

Capture follows a simple principle:

Seen, Not Solved.

Unknown information remains unknown.

Assumptions remain assumptions.

Evidence remains attached to reality.

---

## Main

Main assembles the House, the Systems and the Home into a living Digital Twin.

Main does not create features.

Main creates understanding.

The Digital Twin is not a drawing.

It is not a report.

It is not a dashboard.

It is a living model of how the building, its systems and the people who live there interact.

Understanding emerges from those interactions.
